# LOCO Cover Art Placeholder

## Instructions for Adding Your Cover Image

1. **Prepare your cover art:**
   - Format: JPG or PNG
   - Recommended size: 1200x1200px (square)
   - File size: Under 2MB for fast loading

2. **Replace this placeholder:**
   - Save your cover image as `loco-cover.jpg`
   - Place it in: `/public/loco-cover.jpg`
   - This will replace the placeholder and show on the landing page

3. **SEO Optimization:**
   - The image will be used for Open Graph and Twitter Card previews
   - Make sure it looks good when shared on social media
   - Consider adding your artist name or song title in the image
